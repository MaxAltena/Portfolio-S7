Assignment

To create a story, I had to have a Protaganist and a media to communicate the story through. So I created both.

Approach

To start off, I created a protaganist to base my story on and create a bigger story around it. After this, I created a media that would fit this story and the way I wanted to communicate it.

Execution

As stated in the approach, I started with making a protaganist based on world I described inside of the Pecha Kucha I created. After the received feedback, I created a more detailed protaganist to communicate the story. This, first, description and it's details can be found within the document.

After creating a protaganist, I started working on the media where this story would later be told. I quiet quickly came to an interactive dairy in which the reader could go through the book and even make choices for the character. It would later become clear that the character would become you, with it's name being yours.

The creation of the media was some work, but I made it work in the end. It represents and actual book and should feel like one when browsing through your story. The story can be found on this website to be browsered with all it's glory.

Reflection

Make and thinking of a story and/or protaganist was harder than I initially thought. It took much more imagination and logic to piece together a character with their emotions to connect to the reader. In addition, not having the input from other students all the time was not ideal to say the least.

Learning outcomes

Creating the story and the media it is depicted on was an experience itself and is an experience for the reader as well. It is different than your average and standard media known as a book or magazine.
