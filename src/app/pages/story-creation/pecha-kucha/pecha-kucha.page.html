Assignment

Make a Pecha Kucha in which you answer the following questions:

  • What is the essence of your subject?
  • Why did you choose this subject?
  • What do you want to achieve with your story?

Approach

To create the present my story with a Pecha Kucha, I was going to create a rough setup of the presentation itself. See what topics I want to touch on and what I want to tell about the content of the story. After this, I will look for imagery to accompany these thoughts within a presentation.

Execution

I first had a rough idea on the way to communicate the story. From this idea I directly started creating the presentation itself with some notes in there. The imagery continued not too long afterwards.

Reflection

The presentation went well, the story and idea behind it were conveyed to the audience. The only thing I would do different next time is my preparation for it. Talking for 20 seconds per slide is a challenge, because of both not over- or under-talking the slide.

After the presentation, feedback was all about the story itself: 'How do they call themselves?', 'What kind of symbolism is within the story?' and 'How should the story end, if it does?'. These questions will be answered with the protaganist where the story already becomes a bit more solid.
