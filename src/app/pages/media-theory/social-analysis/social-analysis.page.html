Assignment

Analyse your favourite social medium you are using the model of the tetrad of media effects. Why are you using it? What are you portraying on the social medium? What parts of you are extended on the medium? What would happen if you would be hacked - how would you feel?

Execution

I only use a few mediums in my daily life. I mainly use WhatsApp to communicate and YouTube and Reddit for entertainment and information. As such, WhatsApp extends my connection to my friends and family while YouTube and Reddit extend me through providing information I find relevant.

If I would get hacked on either YouTube or Reddit, I wouldn't bat a single eye. I couldn't care that much about those two. But WhatsApp. If I get hacked on that, I will lose a lot of communication with for example my girlfriend, family and friends.

What does WhatsApp enhance?

WhatsApp enhances my ability to communicate with other people. Be it because of distance or, like now, because of a pandemic. I can keep updated with my girlfriend, family and friends all through a single medium.

What does WhatsApp make obsolete?

WhatsApp makes the usage of other communication systems obselete and, in a way, makes it obselete to communicate with eachother in real life. Yet, I don't feel like WhatsApp will be replacing my real life anytime soon.

What does WhatsApp retrieve?

WhatsApp retrieves the ability to communicate with others, no matter the distance between eachother. This is a given in today's society, but was not imaginable tens of years ago.

What does WhatsApp flip into?

If WhatsApp would be pushed to the exterme, everyone now communicates exclusively on the medium. No more other apps to schedule an appoinment, simply with WhatsApp. Or possibly even replacing real life? No. I don't think that would happen.

Reflection

Think and rationalizing the reasons and the ways I use certian mediums made me more aware of the advantages and disadvantages of certain mediums. This has indirectly impacted me to create project such as Digital Detox or Detoxers to help me start this process.

In a way, I think we should all lower or minimize the amount of mediums we use and the duration of it. Simply because these advantages or enhancements are only positive when used correctly.
