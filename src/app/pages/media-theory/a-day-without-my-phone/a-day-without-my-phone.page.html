Assignment

Reflect on how you experience a day without your phone. One day from 7am till 12 midnight (all day). NO PHONE! Record what you feel - do - act. For recording use [insert medium of choice here - be aware of your choice though].

Approach

The only correct approach for this is texting your friends and family that you will not be able to communicate with them the upcoming day.

Execution

I disabled my phone the first thing in the morning. It was weird. I kept a small dairy of all the events that happened throughout the day and reflect on these. This made it simple to see what actions I normally would have taken with a phone but was now not able to do.

Conclusion

We need our phone these days. No going around it. Is this really what we want?

Reflection

This was a fun and mostly insightful assignment. I knew we were very attached to our devices. But to this level, I didn't notice. Normal things, like communicating, searching an answer on a question or simply the time the train leaves are now all done through our phone. This made me more aware of the usage and importance of our devices even more, as I already had a good understanding (if I say so myself).

Responsibility

I did this reflection and day without my phone all by myself. My girlfriend did not want to participate (ever).
