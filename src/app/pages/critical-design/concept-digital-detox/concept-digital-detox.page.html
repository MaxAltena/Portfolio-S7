Assignment

To create a concept for my personal project, Woody suggested to mix all of the concepts into a single concept.

Approach

As Woody suggested, I will create a single concept with the most important elements of the different concepts. In addition, I should look at rewarding people instead of punishing them as Woody didn't think punishing would motivate people to use/participate.

Execution

I started fusing the concepts by creating an experience where all these elements come back into. After brainstorming by myself a bit on which elements should be included where, I came up with the Digital Detox concept where getting off your phone is the most important thing in the world. You simply hand in your phone somewhere, whereafter you earn rewards based on how long you do not use your phone. With this assignment
