Assignment

Look at your manifesto and try to extract a topic for your personal project. Make groups and execute a brainstorm on this topic. Do some profound research on your topic and turn your ideas from the brainstorm into 5 concepts for your personal project. Describe them in words and images.

Approach

To start gathering a topic, I will look at my personal manifesto and also my critical problems that I wrote down to see which ones I find most interesting. From here I can execute the brainstorm and create ideas & multiple concepts.

Execution

I firstly took a look at my previous assignments for Critical Design and saw one that stood out: Media consumption. I knew directly that this would become my topic for the brainstorm. After each other group member thought of a topic, we started a group brainstorm.

Within the brainstorm, we had a ton of good, mwah and great ideas. I wrote the ideas for my topic down inside of a brainstorm document so I could reference back to the brainstorm later. I later created a more detailed document with concepts from the ideas. These concepts came forth from the ideas, but were altered a bit so they would better fit the topic.

Conclusion

The concepts created are in line with the topic I chose and the things that I value. This way, I will create a more thought out concept because of it being more in line then anything else. Woody found the concepts to be divers in their nature, but would find it more interesting to see these ideas and concepts combined into a single larger concept.

Reflection

The brainstorm session itself was good, but as time went on, the ideas and concentration of the group went down. Therefor, we now know that we need to take a break earlier to optimize the results of the brainstorm. The ideas gathered still were suffice enough to build upon.

Learning outcomes

As we were now looking for problems around the topic, it showed how we, including myself, were thinking critically. This is a great step into the direction of this subject, as it allowed us to now think more deeply about the issues at hand.

Responsibility

We did a brainstorm with the Additive Industries group. I personally made the brainstorm and the concepts documents.
