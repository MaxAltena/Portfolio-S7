Assignment

Create design probes to gather information about behaviour related to the SDGs. By making these probes, you can gather insights which you can use for brainstorming inspiring ideas.

Approach

I was going to make a design probe with Boris, which was not available at the time of starting this assignment. So I first wrote down some ideas with which we would later settle on checking your fridge and freezer.

Execution

We first created a more of a description of the design probe itself and what information that we wanted to gather with this probe. The initial non-visual design probe concept was written down and documented after which Boris created the first visual design probe. We used this ourselves and I updated the current visualization to be a bit more pleasing and inviting to look at and participate in. This second and final visual design probe is what we sent to the other group with whom we were switching our desing probes with.

Conclusion

The result of the design probe were not necessarily execting and ground-breaking findings, but were insightful for us. We gathered insights such as that the users weren't going to the grocery store within the time period that was given. This is something we need to look at the next time: Match what we expect from the user to what the user actually will do.

Reflection

The usage of the design probe was something I have never done before. It was suprising and fun, because you do become more aware of what you are doing. Simply by giving someone a set task on what they should do or watch already has a big impact on the awareness that it creates.

Responsibility

Boris and me created the design probe together. Boris created the first visual design probe which I improved upon with the final version.
